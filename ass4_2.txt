# Q2(a). Get the names of all the teachers of **'Physics'** department who teach **'Thermodynamics'**.

```sql
SELECT NAME
FROM TEACHER_39
NATURAL JOIN TAUGHTBY_39
NATURAL JOIN SUBJECT_39
WHERE DEPT = 'Physics'
AND SUBTITLE = 'Thermodynamics';
```

---

# Q2(b). Rename the subject **'DBMS'** to **'RDBMS'**.

```sql
UPDATE SUBJECT_39
SET SUBTITLE = 'RDBMS'
WHERE SUBTITLE = 'DBMS';
```

---

# Q2(c). Find all the students who stay in **'Kolkata'** and whose roll number is between **20 and 25**.

```sql
SELECT *
FROM STUDENT_39
WHERE CITY = 'Kolkata'
AND ROLLNO BETWEEN 20 AND 25;
```

---

# Q2(d). Display all the students' information in descending order of their roll number who stay in **'Kolkata'**.

```sql
SELECT *
FROM STUDENT_39
WHERE CITY = 'Kolkata'
ORDER BY ROLLNO DESC;
```
